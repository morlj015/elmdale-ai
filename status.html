How this page works

This page fetches live status data directly from the official status pages of each platform. We check every 60 seconds. Status information is sourced from each provider and reflects what they are reporting publicly.

If a service shows as operational here but your automation is still not working, the issue may be specific to your account or workflow rather than a platform-wide outage. Visit the troubleshooting guide for a full checklist of things to check, or contact us and we will investigate.

Some services do not publish a machine-readable status feed. For those, we link directly to their status page so you can check manually.
